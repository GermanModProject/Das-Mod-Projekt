What is German Space Alliance?

German Space Alliance is a common project from Modders of the German Kerbal Space Program Community. Our aim was to join forces and to create a Mod for the Englisch Community as thanks that you also share your mods with us! Of course this mod wouldn`t exist without the German Community, but our thanks is for you dear Community!
